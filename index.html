Stanley Bassett

Stanley Stuart Bassett Sr was born in California on October 5, 1937. He graduated from high school at the Riverside, California School for the Deaf excelling in many sports setting many school records. He married Faith Warner and had an incredibly successful marriage. He was a dedicated father, hard worker and loving grandfather, who loved to play and tease all the grandchildren.
